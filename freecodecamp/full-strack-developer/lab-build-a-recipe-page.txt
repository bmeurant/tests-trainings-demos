    <h1>Quiche lorraine</h1>
    <p>Richly flavored with Gruyère cheese and smoky bacon, quiche Lorraine is one of the most satisfying dishes imaginable.</p>
    <h2>Ingredients</h2>
    <ul>
      <li>Thick-cut bacon</li>
      <li>Shallots</li>
      <li>Eggs</li>
      <li>Heavy cream</li>
    </ul>
    <h2>Instructions</h2>
    <ol>
      <li>Dice the bacon. Place the diced bacon in a medium nonstick sauté pan over medium heat. Cook, stirring occasionally, until crisp, about 10 minutes.</li>
      <li>In a medium bowl, whisk the eggs, and then the heavy cream, salt, cayenne pepper, and nutmeg.</li>
      <li>Spread the shallots evenly over the bottom of the cooked crust.</li>
      <li>Top with half of the bacon.</li>
      <li>Then all of the Gruyère.</li>
      <li>Pour the egg/cream mixture over top.</li>
    </ol>
